About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral associate in the DiVE Virtual Reality Research Lab at Duke University. I am investigating next generation public-safety user interfaces. I received my Ph.D. (2018) and Master's (2014) degrees in Computer Science from the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS), and a Bachelors degree in Digital Technologies from the University of Caxias do Sul (2011).
