I'm a Ph.D assistant at the Graphics, Interaction and Visualization Group of the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS) advised by Prof. Dr. Anderson Maciel. I have received my Master degree in Computer Science in 2014 from UFRGS and Bachelor degree in Digital Technologies from the University of Caxias do Sul (2011). I'm supported by the National Council for Scientific and Technological Development (CNPq).
