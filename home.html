About Me

I'm a Ph.D candidate at the Institute of Informatics of the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS). advised by Prof. Dr. Anderson Maciel. I have received my Master degree in Computer Science in 2014 from the UFRGS and Bachelor degree in Digital Technologies from the University of Caxias do Sul. I have participated on internship under Dr. Diana Domingues supervision for four years and under Dr. Anderson Maciel supervision for one year, being supported by the National Council for Scientific and Technological Development (IC/CNPq scholarship). My research interests are human-computer interaction and interfaces, medical applications in computer graphics, digital arts, image processing, and virtual, mixed and augmented reality.
